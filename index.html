About

I'm a data analyst focused on the game industry, with years of experience breaking down online gaming — competitive and casual — to understand the main drivers of player engagement, and, most importantly, fun.

My work spans data sourcing (vendor APIs, web scraping), modeling, visualization and analysis. I use modern technology and industry knowledge to help stakeholders make informed decisions at both product and enterprise level.

I teamed up with Dmitri Williams (Professor, USC & Griffin Scientific) to implement, test and analyze a powerful new tool called Social Value. In short — it tracks player interactions to calculate the influence one player has over another.
